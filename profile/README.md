## Our Mission

> [!NOTE]
> To create an inclusive, friendly, and collaborative space where members can:
>
> - **Learn** modern technologies through hands-on collaboration.
> - **Build** real-world projects with strong engineering practices.
> - **Share** knowledge, feedback, and opportunities for growth.

---

## What We Do

We engage in a wide variety of activities to help builders and learners grow:

- **Software Engineering:** Web, mobile, and software development collaboration.
- **UI/UX Design:** User interface and user experience design review and feedback.
- **AI & Machine Learning:** Exploration of artificial intelligence, LLMs, and data science.
- **Cybersecurity:** Sharing awareness, best practices, and secure coding concepts.
- **Open Source:** Collaborating on open-source projects and contributing code.
- **Programming Support:** General technical discussions and troubleshooting help.
- **Learning Resources:** Curated guides, documentation, and industry updates.
- **Project Showcases:** Demos of member-built projects with constructive peer feedback.

---

## Community Standards

We believe in fostering a supportive environment built on key values:

- **Constructive Communication:** Always respectful, welcoming, and friendly.
- **Curiosity & Mentorship:** Encouraging continuous learning and helping others grow.
- **Collaboration:** Valuing teamwork and sharing over solo competition.
- **Engineering Excellence:** Emphasizing code quality, clear documentation, and maintainability.
